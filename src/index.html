STUDIO GHIBLI

Studio Ghibli is a Japanese animation
film studio headquartered in Koganei, Tokyo.
It's best known for its animated feature films,
and has also produced several short films and
television commercials. The studio's mascot
and most recognizable symbol is the character
Totoro, a giant cat-like spiritfrom the studio's
1988 film My Neighbor Totoro.
